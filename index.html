⚙️ Buffer Quantity Settings

⚠️ Buffer qty is the minimum threshold. Below buffer = amber warning. Zero = red critical. Set a global default here, or override per drug on receipt.
✅ In Stock — Qty above buffer level
⚠️ At Buffer — Qty at or near buffer threshold (amber row)
🚨 Critical / Zero — Zero or critically low (red row)
☠️ Expired — Past expiry date (red row, purple pill)
